Resources for Anxiety

Symptoms of Anxiety

All of us have struggled with anxiety at some point in time. It is a very difficult mental illness as it is one of the most common ones to afflict us. Some common symtpoms of anxiety include:

  • Feeling nervous or tense
  • Trouble concentrating
  • Restlessness
  • Irritability
  • Feeling on-edge
  • Having difficulty controlling worry

Coping Mechanisms

Anxiety varies per person and so do coping mechanisms. Below is a list of healthy coping mechanisms that are universally used for treatment of anxiety:

  • Talk with Someone. Talking with someone is often helpful because while walking through your thoughts, you will find yourself feeling a little bit more calm.
  • Take a Short Break. Though it may seem contradictory when you have a lot on your plate, it does help to take a 5-10 minute break. Focus on your breathing during this time and do something such as meditation to slow your mind and heart rate.
  • Listen to Music. Listening to classical, soft pop, and other similar genres music are proven to lower anxiety. See some of the resources below for some playlists.
