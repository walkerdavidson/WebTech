Meet the Founders

Jack Shangold, CEO

Jack Shangold is from Middletown, New Jersey, and is a college student at Babson College studying entrepreneurship. After going through struggles with his own mental health, Jack wanted to create a virtual support system for those going through similar struggles.

Walker Davidson, COO

Walker is from Atlanta, Georgia, and is a college student at Babson College studying Operations and Information Management. Walker joined Tal immediately upon hearing the idea from Jack. Walker struggled with mental health throughout his first and second year of college and wanted to help other students who are struggling with the same things.
