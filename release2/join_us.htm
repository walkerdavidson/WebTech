We Are a Movement


Mental health affects everyone, and we mean everyone. Sadly, many people still carry stigmas around mental illnesses without realizing that everyone has experienced a mental illness at some point in time.

Tal is more than just an app. We are a community and a movement and we would love for you to join us. Changing the stigmas around mental health takes all of us, together.

Become an Ambassador

Ambassadors are crucial for spreading the word. As an ambassador, we'll send you materials to spread the word about Tal, and we'll send you cool gear as we grow. Our goal is to have an ambassdor in every state and then in every college and university. We want you to join us on this journey.
